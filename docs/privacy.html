Sermon App Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Sermon App is a recording app for saving, organizing, and sharing audio recordings.

Information We Collect

When you use Sermon App, we may collect account information such as your email address and basic sign-in details when you sign in with Google. We may also store recordings, recording titles, dates, folders, tags, sharing details, and other metadata you add in the app.

How We Use Information

We use this information to provide the app’s main features, including account access, cloud backup, organizing recordings, syncing your data, and sharing recordings with people you choose.

Audio Recordings

Your recordings may be saved locally on your device and may also be uploaded to cloud storage when cloud upload is enabled. Recordings are used only to provide the app’s recording, storage, playback, and sharing features.

Sharing

If you share a recording with another person, that person may be able to access the recording and related details you choose to share.

Third-Party Services

Sermon App may use third-party services for sign-in, cloud storage, and app infrastructure, including Google sign-in and backend/cloud storage providers.

Data Selling and Tracking

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising tracking.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account data or recordings by contacting us at pshichenkoj@gmail.com.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at pshichenkoj@gmail.com.
